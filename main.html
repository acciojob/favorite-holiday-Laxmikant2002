Paris, France


Paris is known as the city of lights and love, offering a blend of history, art, and culture that attracts millions of visitors every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue for shopping and culture.
  5. Seine River Cruise – Scenic boat tours through the heart of Paris.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky breakfast pastries.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Escargot – A French delicacy of cooked snails.
