Paris, France


Paris is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world, known for its romantic atmosphere, rich culture, and iconic landmarks. Visitors enjoy history, art, food, and unforgettable views.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most famous landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the Mona Lisa and countless artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a world-renowned avenue for shopping and leisure.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – offering breathtaking views of the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery and flaky French pastries.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes served sweet or savory.
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate French desserts.
  • Escargot – a traditional French delicacy.
  • French Cheese – varieties like Brie, Camembert, and Roquefort.
